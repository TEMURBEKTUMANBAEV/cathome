MY HISTORY

ReManga

Good evening, everyone. My name is Temirbek. I'm 18 years old. Now I live in the city of Karakol and study at the DevX IT school. At the lesson on Soft-skills, I did not understand the topic about Blockchain. And when I got home, I started looking for all possible topics about blockchain. And after reading all the sites about blockchain. I concluded that the blockchain is a database with transactions consisting of a sequentially built chain of digital blocks, each of which stores information about the previous and next blocks. Blockchain technology is an advanced database mechanism that allows you to organize an open exchange of information within a business network. The blockchain database stores data in blocks linked together in a chain. Currently, blockchain technologies are used in areas such as financial transactions, user identification or the creation of cybersecurity technologies, and are also relevant for banking institutions and government organizations. According to this criterion , blockchains can be divided into four groups: 1)Public decentralized blockchains. 2)Public blockchains with delegated management. 3)Private controlled blockchains. 4) State blockchains. Basically, government agencies and banks use blockchains. Rarely used for cybersecurity. So let's get to the point about blockchain — it is a developing innovative technology that is being implemented in various industries. Well, this is my little presentation about the blockchain has come to an end thank you for your attention and bye to everyone!
